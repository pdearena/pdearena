What is PDEArena?


An easy-to-use, scalable framework for PDE Surrogate learning:
▶️ Simple and flexible codebase
▶️ Rapid implementation and evaluation of research ideas
▶️ Large collection of baseline results and associated implementations
▶️ Supports efficient scalable distributed training, powered by PyTorch Lightning
